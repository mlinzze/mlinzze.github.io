I am a passionate teacher of economics and environmental sciences. My main expertise is in environmental economics, development economics, sustainable development, and environmental change. At LSE, I have been teaching on the following courses:

  • Environmental Economics (2021/22)
  • Economic Geography of Trade, Production, and Development (2021/22)
  • Sustainable Development (2020/21)
  • Environmental Change: Past, Present, and Future (2020/21 and 2021/22)

Office hours can be booked via StudentHub.

My teaching has received very positive evaluations (available upon request).

Previously I taught courses at the University of Hamburg and worked as a course leader at the summer school NachhaltigkeitsAkademie.
