<h1 id="foo">Teaching</h1>

Manuel is an experienced teacher of economics and environmental sciences. His main expertise is in environmental economics, development economics, sustainable development, and environmental change. A list of past courses can be found below.

London School of Economics and Political Science:
- [Environmental Economics](https://www.lse.ac.uk/resources/calendar2020-2021/courseGuides/GY/2020_GY222.htm) (2021/22)
- [Economic Geography of Trade, Production, and Development](https://www.lse.ac.uk/resources/calendar2020-2021/courseGuides/GY/2020_GY209.htm) (2021/22)
- [Sustainable Development](https://www.lse.ac.uk/resources/calendar2020-2021/courseGuides/GY/2020_GY121.htm) (2020/21)
- [Environmental Change: Past, Present, and Future](https://www.lse.ac.uk/resources/calendar/courseGuides/GY/2020_GY120.htm) (2020/21 and 2021/22)

Manuel also taught courses at the University of Hamburg and worked as a course leader at the summer school [NachhaltigkeitsAkademie](https://jgw-ev.de/nachhaltigkeitsakademie/).

Manuel won the Class Teacher Award at LSE in 2022.
